Frequently asked questions

Can I order from multiple stalls at once?

Yes. Add items from any stall in the same hawker centre to one cart and check out once. Each stall receives its own order.

When do I collect my food?

You have 15 minutes after your order is marked ready. You'll get a Telegram notification the moment it's done.

How do refunds work?

Refunds are handled directly by the vendor through their dashboard. You'll receive a Telegram update when a refund is processed.

How will I know my order is ready?

Hawkr sends you a Telegram message as soon as the vendor marks your order as ready.

What happens to my data?

We store only what's needed to process your orders. We don't sell data, and we don't run ads.

Who pays for HawkerOS?

Operators of the hawker centre pay for HawkerOS as a whole, benefitting vendors by giving them more outreach and giving consumers a simple, efficient, familiar way to order. We aim to be a non-profit, fuelling Singapore's hawker culture.
